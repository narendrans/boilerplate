Trim YouTube Videos


1.Drag the below bookmarklet to your bookmark toolbar.

YoutTube Trim

2.Click the bookmark when you watch a YouTube video to trim it.

3.Copy the url and share it!


  • The url should be like -> https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z-TOHc7I1pc
  • Specify time range as mm:ss,mm:ss. Example: 00:34,1:23 (if minutes is less than 1, 0 or 00 is needed)
